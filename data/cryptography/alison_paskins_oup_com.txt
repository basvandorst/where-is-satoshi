Oxford University Press launched Journal of Cybersecurity (JCS), a new open access publication in Autumn 2014.
Professor David Pym (UCL) and Professor Tyler Moore (SMU) are Editors-in-Chief of the journal, and JCS has reputable members of the field as Area Editors and Advisory Board Members ( JCS is a multi-/inter-disciplinary journal, premised on the belief that computer science-based approaches, while necessary, are not sufficient to tackle cybersecurity challenges, and that instead, scholarly contributions from a range of disciplines are needed to understand the human aspects of cybersecurity.
