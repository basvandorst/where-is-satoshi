Add your agent or company listings to your existing website!
Place your personal or company listings and photos on your website. Free.  Keep your listings updated and current on your website. Free. No gimmicks.  No mid-game rule changes.  No kidding around.
Here's how it works:
1. Go to  and get a password. Click on Sign Up.  2. After you have your password, click on Add / Edit Listings and enter your listings into Property Line's database.
3. Click on Listing Display Classic to get your special line of code for your listings or your company's listings.
4. Email the line of code to your webmaster to paste into your site. (Your code is always available on Property Line under your password.)
That's it. Property Line's Listing Display Classic delivers a special piece of programming that retrieves your listings from the Property Line site and displays them in a summary format on your site.  Each listing has a one-click detail photo report.  New listings?  Add them to Property Line and they will update automatically on your site. Free.
Listing Display Classic is designed to fit into a variety of site designs. Listing Display Classic is absolutely free - now AND in the future. No mid-game rule changes here.  If you're looking for a listing display that matches your site exactly, try Listing Display Pro. Pro lets you choose custom colors and fonts and even gives you a search engine on your site for your listings.
