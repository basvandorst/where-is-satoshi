Las Vegas Central Reservations Corp. and its Subsidiary 1-800-Las-Vegas Go Public Monday September 18, 9:45 am ET
LAS VEGAS, NEVADA--(MARKET WIRE)--Sep 18, 2006 -- Las Vegas Central Reservations Corp. and its subsidiary 1-800-LAS-VEGAS (Other OTC:LVCC. PK - News), a Las Vegas based company focused on travel reservation services announced today that it has become a public entity and will trade on the Pink Sheets with a symbol of LVCC.
"We are absolutely thrilled to be entering the public capital market. Our arrival provides us with the impetus to challenge such rivals as Expedia and Travelocity and prove our service and prices for Las Vegas travel are the best anywhere," commented Douglas Blair, CEO of Las Vegas Central Reservations Corp (LVCC). LVCC operates as 1-800-LAS-VEGAS and 1-800-LAS-VEGAS. com providing reservations for Hotels, Flight Packages, Show Tickets, Night Clubs and Golf Tee Times. Currently there are no other reservation services branding a toll free number for Las Vegas based travel and entertainment.
"Considering Las Vegas is the number one travel destination on the planet, with no end in sight to its runaway visitor growth, LVCC is positioned to gain significant growth and market share through its 1-800-LAS-VEGAS and 1800lasvegas. com marketing initiatives," explains Blair.
