We have just launched a new Portal Community with a wide variety of Free
tools and resources for nonprofits.  The site is at All nonprofits receive:
:) Free Multiple Listings in our Nonprofit Search Engine/Directory
:) Free Unlimited Auction Listings - Never a charge to List, Buy or Sell!
:) Free Unlimited Classified Ads (You can even add pictures)
:) Free Message Boards for Nonprofit Issues
:) Free Chat Rooms for Nonprofit Discussion
:) Free Directory/Search Engine of Useful Links to:
    :-) Government resources
    :-) Free stuff for nonprofits
    :-) Informational & Educational links
    :-) Links to Services & Product providers & more
:) Free Web Page
:) Free Membership into our Charitable Marketing Alliance
:) Free Email
:) Free Internet Service
:) Discount Domain Hosting
:) Discount Domain Registrations
There are still more features at the site and more coming soon.  If you know
of any useful links for nonprofits, please add them also.  Please check it
out for yourself and let us know what you think.  There is no catch.  There
is no fine print.  This site is Free because we appreciate all the wonderful
things Nonprofits do to make this a better world.
If you find it useful, please link to our site from yours so that other
nonprofits can find us as well.  Be sure an add your nonprofit in the
categories that you feel are relevant.  All Nonprofit Links will be
validated within 24 hours.
