You can use "--export-secret-keys" for that, e.g.:
gpg -a --export-secret-keys However, I run into problems when using the (german) international
PGP-Freeware-Version 6.5.1: Importing Public keys from GnuPG works just
fine, but when trying to import a secret key I always get "sizeAdvise-Versprechen nicht eingehalten" (Did not keep sizeAdvise-Promise).
Importing secret keys generated by PGP into GnuPG works, though.
