Quoting David K. Trudgett (dkt on Tue, Sep 25, 2001 at 05:19:43AM +0200:
Found that on a mailing list ages ago for my .procmailrc:
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - # ----------------------------------------------------------------------
# PGP to PGP-MIME conversion
# Add a "Content-Type: application/pgp" header so Mutt will know the
# mail is encrypted.
* !^Content-Type: message/
* !^Content-Type: multipart/
* !^Content-Type: application/pgp
        :0 fBw
        * ^-----BEGIN PGP MESSAGE-----
        * ^-----END PGP MESSAGE-----
 formail \
            -i "Content-Type: application/pgp; format=text; x-action=encrypt"
        :0 fBw
        * ^-----BEGIN PGP SIGNED MESSAGE-----
        * ^-----BEGIN PGP SIGNATURE-----
        * ^-----END PGP SIGNATURE-----
 formail \
            -i "Content-Type: application/pgp; format=text; x-action=sign"
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
