                                                  New Orders, Extending Global ePoint's Worldwide Leadership CITY OF INDUSTRY, Calif.—Dec. 10, 2004--Global ePoint, Inc. (Nasdaq:GEPT - News), an innovator of homeland security solutions in aviation, law enforcement and high-volume contract manufacturing, announced today that the Company's AirWorks division has received orders for 12 Cockpit Door Surveillance Systems from European airline Germanwings. Nine of the systems have already been shipped and the remainder will be delivered by year end. The announcement was made by Toresa Lou, Chief Executive Officer of Global ePoint.
