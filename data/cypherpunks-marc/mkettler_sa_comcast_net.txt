How did you whitelist? merely a whitelist_from??? That's pretty much a known-bad-idea. At least use whitelist_from_rcvd for such things.
whitelist_from is pretty much a tool of last-resort. You're always vulnerable to forgery with it, which is why whitelist_from_rcvd was created. Spammers have been abusing people foolish enough to whitelist_from * for at least 2 years now, and probably much longer.
