Welcome to the Cypherpunks Distributed Remailer (CDR)
Cypherpunks is a high-traffic, unmoderated forum for the discussion of
encryption, politics and technology in general.  The CDR is a network of
mail list managers intended, for historical reasons, to decentralize control
of the list such that moderation or censorship of the forum becomes unfeasible.
TO SUBSCRIBE to Cypherpunks, one should send a message to ONE of the following
The message should read, "subscribe cypherpunks".  Due to the high traffic and
low signal-to-noise ratio of Cypherpunks digestified, filtered, and moderated
versions of the list are available.  To subscribe to a digestified version of
Cypherpunks, send a message to the following address:
The message should read, "subscribe cypherpunks-digest".
TO UNSUBSCRIBE from Cypherpunks, one should send a message to the following The message should read, "unsubscribe cypherpunks", or, if you are writing from
an address other than the one from which you are subscribed, the message should
read, "unsubscribe cypherpunks ".
If you intend on subscribing to the Cypherpunks list, saving this message for
future reference might not be a bad idea.
