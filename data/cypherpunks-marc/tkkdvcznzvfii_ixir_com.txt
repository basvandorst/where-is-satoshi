Not Prudy, for the poor little thing had grown so lame by this time, that she was unable to bear her weight on her feet, much less to walk into the nursery.
 This Prudy herself could propel with only the outlay of a very little strength; but there were days when she did not care to sit in it at all.
 The coal glowed in the grate with a splendid blaze: all the gas-burners were lighted, and so were everybody's eyes.
