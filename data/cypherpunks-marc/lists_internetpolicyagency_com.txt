As is common in most industries there are expressions in the world of Internet Governance that are jargon, and have agreed meanings in that "Illegal Material" is reserved for content which is illegal to possesses and/or distribute (even if, and possibly even more so, if you originated "Harmful Material" is content which is legal to possess but is nevertheless regarded by many as immoral or highly undesirable within some framework of commonly held values.
"Infringing Material" is content which is held without a legitimate rightsholder's permission.
