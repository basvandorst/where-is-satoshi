Welcome to the Agilent Technologies Semiconductor Products website! We would like to thank you for becoming a registered user. Our site is designed and created with you - the Engineer - in mind. As a registered user, you will be able to access the following services:
- Easy, fast access to Product Information and Literature pages - each page lists all the  information for a particular product, its associated literature (datasheets, application notes etc), design tools, product notifications and associated products, all in one place. From this page you can click:
1) Add to Links: This button allows you to add the selected product number to your links folder. Add to links works very similar to the favorite/bookmark function on our browser and allows easy access to the pages/documents that are visited frequently. 2) Keep me Updated: This button allows you to receive the product updates, product change notifications for the products selected and receive obsolescence notifications and the replacement parts information
3) Buy Now: This button allows you hassle free shopping and a quick purchase of small quantity of product by credit card from one of your preferred Channel Partners
- Product Selection Tool allows you to search the product/s by market, application and/or technical specification in an easy and efficient manner
- If you are interested in a specific market and/or application click on our Design Folder. For your convenience, we have placed all information for a single topic in this folder. It includes links to the information available on our website for the same subject as well links to related websites.
- To support your design needs click on Design Tools, an easy way to access all design support utilities, models, reference designs, evaluation kits etc.
- Need to know the product lead times? Click our Order Fulfillment Services center to get information related to product availability
