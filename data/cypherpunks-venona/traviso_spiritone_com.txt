--- 1.Point the mouse at the desktop and click the right mouse button. Choose "New" from the resulting pop-up menu, and then choose "Folder." 2.A new folder will appear on the desktop, with the temporary name "New Folder." Change the folder's name by typing "and now, the moment you've all been waiting for" (do not type the quotation marks), and pressing the Enter key. 3.Point at the folder and click the right mouse button to display its pop-up context menu. Choose the Rename command, and type "we proudly present for your viewing pleasure" (again, without the quotation marks). Press the Enter key. 4.Once again, right-click the folder and choose the Rename command. This time, type "The Microsoft Windows 95 Product Team!" (again, without the quotation marks). Press the Enter key. 5.Double-click on the folder to open it.
