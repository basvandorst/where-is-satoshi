It is imperative therefore that future development & research and protections of future versions of TOR & other hidden networks create protections against traffic analysis attacks that make traffic indistinguishable from other traffic making metadata analysis hard & also traffic inspection hard also & also future protocols and developments also need to adopt larger bandwidths to cater for a more rich and vibrant content that can handle future media & demand.
