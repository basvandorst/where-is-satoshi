While on the topic of "free" web servers, one might check out Xitami.
I had used this product several months ago, not related to the tor
experiment, and ( at that time ) had found it was a quick way to start
serving pages.  It has simple installation and configuration.  Works on
windows and I see now they have binaries available for M$ and source
code available for both M$ and Linux.  I've read the license at
  I'm not an
attorney so cannot say if the license is FSF compatible, but on first
glance it looks decent.
