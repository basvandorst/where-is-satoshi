This information can be used against you/your Hidden Services to find
your address. I recommend wiping your Tor's state.
Knowing the guard of a HS can have high impact on anonymity.
On Debian, this should be /var/lib/tor. Backup your HS keys, you want
to save those but the rest should be blown away to protect anonymity.
